## Problem

Medical-billing claims contain improper, miscoded, or non-covered charges. Manual
audit is slow and inconsistent; the inputs are multimodal (structured records plus
unstructured PDFs and scanned images) and the rules are dense. The goal is an agent
that flags improper claims with **high recall** (don't miss bad claims) while keeping
**cost-per-claim** low enough to run at scale.

## Approach

An agentic system built on the Claude Agent SDK and orchestrated with LangGraph:
ingest the claim, extract structured fields from documents, retrieve relevant
coding/coverage rules via hybrid RAG over pgvector, reason over them, and emit
explainable flags with citations. Cheap/easy cases are routed to a small model
(supplied by `fine-tune-lab`) to control cost.
