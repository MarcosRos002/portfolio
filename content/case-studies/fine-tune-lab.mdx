## Problem

Large models are accurate but expensive. Many requests are "easy" and don't
need the biggest model. We want a small model that matches the teacher on the
target task at a fraction of the cost and latency.

## Approach

LoRA/QLoRA fine-tuning and distillation from a larger teacher onto a small
student, evaluated against the teacher on the target distribution. The winning
checkpoint becomes the cheap tier in claims-auditor's model router.
